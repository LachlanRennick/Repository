Target Audience

The key demographic for the new website is for a younger age group; approximately the 16-25 year old age group. This age group has been specified due to CossiePlay's desire for a younger audience. The website's design will focus heavily on the colours violet through to black in order to attract this demographic.

Purple is a key point in the website's colour, as it is a colour associated with imaginative thought; as the client operates a cosplay store, it makes sense to use colours that encourage imaginative thought, as it aids the audience in thought regarding the costume. Violet is essentially the same as purple; however, due to it being a lighter shade of purple, it can be used when less intensity is required. On the inverse, black and darker shaes of purple are also a suitable range of colours. Dark purple can give more intense imaginative thought, allowing further immersion.
